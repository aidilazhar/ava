Information Collected on our Site When you interact with the Site, you may provide personal information to AVAVA in a variety of ways, such as when you request information from us, complete a registration form, request a case study, apply for a job, or initiate other transactions. When you do so, you may submit certain information to us, such as your name, email address, job title, company information, mailing address, and phone number.

We may use this information collected on the Site for a variety of purposes:

Sending you information or materials you may request Responding to your questions and concerns Providing you a newsletter or other marketing communications Operating and improving the Site Improving our products and services and our marketing efforts Conducting research and analysis Processing and delivering contest entries and rewards Enabling you to post your resume, search job postings, and contact us or be contacted by us Linking or combining your information with other personal information we may receive from third parties, to help better understand you and serve you better

When you visit the Site, some information is automatically collected. For example, we collect information on the pages you have visited and the contents of those pages, any information you input, your referring URL or IP address, the time of your visit and related session information, your requested page, browser type, USR agent, status of request (successful reply), cookies, number of bytes in the response, and query string. We use this data to better understand usage of the Site in the aggregate so that we know what areas of our Site users prefer. This information is stored in log files and is used for aggregated and statistical reporting.

Cookies The Site uses cookies, which are small text files placed on your computer by a website. Cookies enable AVAVA to track usage patterns and deliver customized content to users. These cookies cannot read information from your hard drive. AVAVA uses cookies to collect and store your User ID, which is not personally identifiable to you. The information is only used to identify repeat visitors to the Site. The Site also uses temporary session cookies to assign and store a Session ID to you so that you can move uninterrupted between areas of our Web Site. AVAVA also uses the Session ID for internal reporting purposes. This Session ID is not personally identifiable to you and this type of cookie does not leave any retrievable information on your hard drive. The User ID does not have an expiration date. Session IDs expire when the browser is closed.

The Site may also use 3rd party vendor re-marketing tracking cookies, including the Google Adwords tracking cookie. This allows AVAVA to make special offers and continue to market our products across the Internet to those who have shown interest on the Site. As always, we respect your privacy and are not collecting any identifiable information through the use of Google’s or any other third party remarketing system. Users may opt out of the use of Google cookies at the following URL: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Please note that clicking a link within a AVAVA email may result in a cookie being placed on your computer/device. If you do not wish to receive a cookie from a link in an email or on the AVAVA Web Site, you may, depending on your browser version, elect to block all cookies. Most browsers have an option for turning off the cookie feature, which will prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, as well as (depending on the sophistication of your browser software) allow you to decide on acceptance of each new cookie in a variety of ways. We recommend that you leave the cookies activated in order to access optimal features on the Site.

Certain features on the Site may be provided by third party services. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing that feature, not this privacy policy.
